1. COVID-19 Dashboard




Description:
The SARS-CoV-2 virus is the infectious illness known as coronavirus disease (COVID-19). The majority of virus-infected individuals will have mild to severe respiratory disease and will recover without the need for special care. However, some people will get severe illnesses and need to see a doctor. Serious sickness is more likely to strike older persons and those with underlying medical conditions, including cancer, diabetes, cardiovascular disease, or chronic respiratory diseases. COVID-19 can cause anyone to get ill or pass away at any age.
